Exhibitions & Collections

Throughout his career Strautmanis participated in many group shows as well as more than a hundred solo shows throughout his life.

Strautmanis' paintings are included in many notable collections, including the Minneapolis Institute of Arts, the Herbert F. Johnson Museum of Cornell University, the Kunsthaus in Zurich and the Latvian Museum of Art in Riga, Latvia.
